---
description: Adversarially stress-test a venture's assumptions and evidence
argument-hint: [venture-slug]
---

Dispatch the **`assumption-red-teamer`** agent to stress-test venture **$ARGUMENTS** like a skeptical
investor and a disciplined co-founder.

It should: separate facts from assumptions across the workspace; rank the riskiest assumptions by
importance × uncertainty; hunt the classic self-deceptions (solution-love, advocacy interviews,
confirmation/selection bias, vanity validation, inflated TAM, COCA undercount, LTV off revenue,
ignoring "do nothing", moat-mistaken-for-Core); demand the cheapest falsifying test (with a threshold)
for each top risk; and fact-check specific external claims.

Return a verdict (proceed / proceed-with-tests / stop-and-fix), the top 3–5 risks with tests, the
hardest investor questions, and what's genuinely strong. Then offer to record the key assumptions and
their tests into the venture's `assumptions.md`.
