Use the **market-research** skill to research: **$ARGUMENTS**

1. Clarify the segment in DE terms (end user + application). Dispatch the **`market-researcher`** agent
   for a sourced secondary landscape (structure, players, trends, sizing inputs, watering holes, named
   prospects to interview).
2. Lead with primary research: if the goal is customer discovery, dispatch the
   **`customer-discovery-analyst`** to produce an interview guide and to synthesize any existing
   interview notes. Reinforce inquiry-not-advocacy.
3. Write secondary sources (with provenance) into the venture's `research/`, interview material into
   `interviews/`, and the synthesized segment/profile into `01-who-is-your-customer.md`. Triangulate
   every number; flag what can only be learned by talking to customers.
