Dispatch the **`assumption-red-teamer`** agent to stress-test venture **$ARGUMENTS** like a skeptical
investor and disciplined co-founder — the consistency gate before `/de-implement`.

It should: separate **facts from assumptions** across the workspace; rank the riskiest by importance
× uncertainty; hunt the classic self-deceptions (inflated/too-broad TAM, COCA undercount, LTV off
revenue instead of gross profit, vanity validation, ignoring the "do nothing" competitor, a moat
mistaken for a Core); and demand the cheapest falsifying test for each top risk.

Return a **verdict** (proceed / proceed-with-tests / stop-and-fix), the hardest investor questions,
and what's genuinely strong. Fold the key risks + their tests into `assumptions.md`. Then show
`/de-next <slug>` — if the verdict is "proceed," continue to **`/de-implement`**.
