Use the **competitive-analysis** skill to map the competitive landscape for **$ARGUMENTS**.

1. Dispatch the **`competitor-analyst`** agent to research direct, indirect, and **status-quo
   ("do nothing")** alternatives — profiles, pricing (sourced), review-site sentiment, and funding.
2. Build the **2×2 positioning map** on the persona's **top two purchasing criteria** and a feature
   matrix; identify the white space your **Core (Step 10)** can defend.
3. Frame everything in customer **benefits**, never just features; never conclude "no competitors."
   Write profiles into `competitors/`, the matrix into `competitors/matrix.md`, and the position into
   `02-what-can-you-do.md` (Step 11). Flag if you are not clearly upper-right.
