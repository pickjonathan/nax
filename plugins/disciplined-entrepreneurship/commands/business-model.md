Use the **go-to-market** skill to design the business model and pricing for venture **$ARGUMENTS**.

1. Dispatch the **`business-model-architect`** agent to recommend a model from the 17 DE archetypes
   (justified against the four lenses) and a **value-based** first-pass price (a fraction of the
   quantified Step 8 value, segmented, with lighthouse-customer terms).
2. Sanity-check against the principles: a model is not a price, "free" needs a capture path, never
   set a high public price then discount publicly, mind the customer's budget cycle (capex/opex).
3. Write the chosen model and pricing into `04-how-you-make-money.md` and update `dashboard.md`. Then
   validate viability with `/unit-economics`.
