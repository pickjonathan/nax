Use the **disciplined-entrepreneurship** skill (`references/theme-5-how-you-build.md`) for venture
**$ARGUMENTS**. This is the "tasks" phase — convert the plan into the riskiest things to test.

1. **Step 20 — Identify key assumptions.** Distil 5–10 load-bearing, leap-of-faith beliefs from the
   plan (pricing acceptance, channel, retention, a critical feasibility, COCA…). Rank by
   **importance × uncertainty**. Record in `assumptions.md`.
2. **Step 21 — Test them.** For each top assumption, design the **cheapest experiment that could
   falsify it**, with a success threshold stated up front (behavioral evidence — pre-orders, LOIs,
   landing-page conversion, supplier quotes — not opinion). Run the cheap ones; record results.

Route any invalidation back to the relevant phase (e.g. a failed pricing test → `/de-plan`). Mark
steps **20–21**. **Gate ahead:** `/de-analyze` (red-team) before building. Finish by showing
`/de-next <slug>`.
