Use the **go-to-market** skill to design how venture **$ARGUMENTS** acquires customers.

1. Map the **DMU (Step 12)** — champion, end user, economic buyer, influencers, veto holders,
   purchasing — as real, named roles, and the **buying process (Step 13)** with sales-cycle length and
   showstoppers. Read `references/theme-3-how-they-acquire.md`.
2. Design the **revenue engine (Step 18)** across short/medium/long horizons and choose a **GTM motion**
   (PLG vs sales-led vs hybrid) consistent with the DMU and the unit economics.
3. Write results into `03-how-they-acquire.md` and `04-how-you-make-money.md`; update `dashboard.md`.
   For business model, pricing, and LTV/COCA, use `/business-model` and `/unit-economics`.
