Disciplined Entrepreneurship. Run **`/de-next {{SLUG}}`** anytime to see the current phase and the

## It's a cycle, not a waterfall
Loop back whenever evidence breaks an earlier assumption — e.g. a clarify interview that kills the
persona sends you back to `/de-specify`. **Never stop doing primary market research.**

## Why the gates exist
They enforce DE's discipline:
- **Clarify before Plan** — you don't get to design the product/business until you've *talked to
  real customers* and confirmed the beachhead is real and homogeneous.
- **Analyze before Implement** — you don't *build* until you've *de-risked* the assumptions the plan
  rests on.

tasks ≈ tasks · analyze ≈ analyze · implement ≈ implement.
